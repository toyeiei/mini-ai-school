# What is Prompting?

Think of an AI like a very knowledgeable assistant who can't read your mind. You need to tell them exactly what you need.

Prompting is the art of asking AI to get the best results.

## Why Does It Matter?

A good prompt gets you:
- Useful answers, not generic responses
- Less back-and-forth
- Actual work done

A vague prompt gets you:
- Generic responses
- Follow-up questions
- Wasted time

## A Simple Example

**Bad prompt:**
```
Write about dogs.
```

**Good prompt:**
```
Write a 200-word introduction about golden retrievers for a first-time dog owner. Include: temperament, exercise needs, and grooming requirements. Tone: friendly and encouraging.
```

See the difference? The second one tells the AI exactly what, how much, and how.

## The Key Idea

You are the expert on what you need. The AI is the expert on how to do it. Your job is to communicate clearly.
